The Challenge

Years of Data.
No Way to Use It.

Most public sector organizations are sitting on years of valuable operational data spread across Google Sheets, archived PDFs, Word documents, and shared drives. When a compliance report is due or leadership asks for a performance summary, staff spend days manually compiling data that should take minutes.

01
Data Fragmentation
Critical information is scattered across dozens of files, formats, and platforms with no unifying layer connecting it all.
02
Manual Reporting Burden
Compliance reports and stakeholder updates require hours of manual data compilation every single time they are needed.
03
No Institutional Memory
When staff members leave, the institutional knowledge embedded in their spreadsheets and files leaves with them.

Intelligent Dashboard

Report Wizard

Security & Compliance

Built to Meet the Standards Public Sector Organizations Are Held To

CCPA & CPRA Compliant

SOW & Procurement Documentation
