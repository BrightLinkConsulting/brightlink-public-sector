Implementing AI in government requires more than capability. It requires compliance, transparency, and documentation that procurement can defend. Every AI system we deploy comes with the security framework and audit trail your agency requires.

CCPA / CPRA Compliant

Data handling practices aligned with California Consumer Privacy Act and California Privacy Rights Act. Documented procedures for data subject requests, retention, and deletion.

NIST-Aligned Encryption

Data encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols aligned with NIST Cybersecurity Framework. No unencrypted data storage.

Access enforced at the data layer. Admins see everything. Department staff see only their data. Every access event logged by user, timestamp, and action.
