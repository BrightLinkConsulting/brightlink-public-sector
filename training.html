Four Weeks. Four Deliverables. Zero Theory-Only Sessions.

Every week produces something concrete. Every session includes hands-on build time. By week four, participants have working systems in production and a roadmap for scaling. This isn't theory. This is implementation.

Week 1

AI Readiness Audit

Every participant completes a structured assessment of their current operations, identifies the highest-ROI automation opportunities, and receives a dollar-figure projection for time and cost savings. This is the document leadership needs to justify the investment.

Week 2

Build Two AI Assistants

Participants build and deploy two working AI company assistants: one for internal operations (SOPs, onboarding, knowledge management) and one for external use (customer support, intake, FAQs). Both are live by end of week.

Week 3

Install a Live Automation

Each participant selects from five proven automation templates and installs at least one fully operational workflow. Options include lead intake, appointment scheduling, document generation, reporting, and client communication sequences.

Week 4

ROI Report + Scaling Roadmap

The final week produces three deliverables: a measured ROI report comparing pre-program metrics to post-implementation results, team training documentation for ongoing management, and a six-month scaling roadmap with prioritized next steps.

Bonus: Week 5

Advanced Multi-Step Automations

Program completers get access to an advanced module covering chained multi-agent workflows, cross-platform integrations, and enterprise-grade automation architecture.
